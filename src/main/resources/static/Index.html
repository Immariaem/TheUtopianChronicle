Fairy Valley
You descend into a sun-dappled valley where the air sparkles with what can only be described as magic made visible. Tiny winged creatures flit between flowers that glow with inner light, and the sound of tinkling laughter fills the air. The fairies of the Enchanted Forest make their home here, and they seem distressed about something. Several cluster together, pointing toward the west and speaking in urgent, chiming voices.
Forest Entrance
You stand at the threshold of the Enchanted Forest, your father's leather-bound book clutched tightly in your hands. The trees here seem to lean inward, curious about the newcomer. Sunlight filters through the canopy in golden shafts, illuminating dancing motes of pollen and perhaps something more magical. This is where your journey truly begins, where you step from the ordinary world into the extraordinary, following the path your father walked three years ago.
